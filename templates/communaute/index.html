Who We Are

A Multilingual Community

We are brothers and sisters centered on Christ and the Bible. We stand on the ground that all regenerated Christians are one.

We emphasize experiencing Jesus Christ subjectively rather than merely as doctrine, and living together as Christians in unity as the Body of Christ.
